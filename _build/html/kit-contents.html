Kit Contents¶

Each UV Transilluminator kit contains the following components:

  1. Acrylic enclosure parts and assembled lid;
  2. 3 bags of hardware;
  3. Mini-screwdriver;
  4. Ballast with lampholder;
  5. UVB bulb;
  6. UV Transilluminator glass (8.25 cm square);
  7. Power connector with on/off switch;
  8. 6’ power cord
